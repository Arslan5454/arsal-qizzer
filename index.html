Services

Leasing

Lease Inquiry
      Lease Inquiry is the form where Inquiry is posted by a customer is added. The admin of system adds the inquiry posted by a customer on this form by adding his details i.e.
  • Customer name
  • Father name
  • Date (date of inquiry)
  • Image
  • Cnic
  • Personal Contact
  • Office Number
  • Office Address
  • Occupation
  • Salary
  • Inquiry Officer (the officer who is in-charge of inquiry of case)
  • Marketing Officer (the officer who is in-charge of marketing and tag to case)
  • Processing Fee type (optional fee, if selected adding Process fee amount is necessary)
  • Process Fee (Amount of process fee)
Add Guarantor
  • Guarantor is the person responsible for guaranty of customer for which case is adding.
  • Guarantor can also be the existing customer in the system but not necessarily.
  • Guarantor has the same details to add as of customer for approving the case i-e Name, father name, Cnic, Personal contact, Email, Office number, Occupation, Salary etc. Relationship with Customer is required to add.
  • A case can have multiple guarantors.
  • A case cannot be added for inquiry unless there is at-least one guarantor
  • After adding and saving a customer and Guarantor without any error, a case no. is generated by system, which will be used for further processing the inquiry.
Case Approval
      On case approval form, first case no. should be assigned to a customer by clicking on label of Assign Case no. to Customer. On clicking the label, a model opens in which first you need to select your case no. (Generated on Add Lease inquiry form after adding inquiry details), and Customer name (name of customer who you need to tag case no. to).
There are three steps of approving case. First is Pending.
  • After tagging customer to a case no. , case is added to Pending List. If the Case is approved (by selecting Approve option) case is moved to next Step i-e Progress. If Rejected, Case is moved to Rejected cases tab.
          The details of case can also be viewed a clicking file option and view the details of Customer and Guarantor(s) added for the case.
  • In Progress tab (step 2 of case approval), A case is again either Approved or Rejected. If rejected case is moved to Rejected tab without further processing, If approved,(by clicking Approved label) case is moved to Approved tab.
  • After a Case is moved to approve tab, it is also hen check from combo box again to confirm Approval. After this case can be moved to Lease Advance for processing the case.
Lease Advance
      On Lease Advance form, a case that is approved is further processed and tagged with Product and also divided into monthly installments and amount is divided into months of lease preferred.
      First, the Lease case is selected from Enter Case Number field. (Approved cases only appear here).First user needs to select Case no. for which Lease Advance needs to be processed. After selecting Case no. , Customer code (COA code of customer) and Customer Name tagged to the case are populated.       Then, Product name is selected for which lease advance needs to be processed.       After product is selected, Sale Price, Total Quantity, item code and Unique ID of the selected product is populated, so that user can:
  • Sale price (Select Sale Price for product)
  • Total Quantity (See Total Quantity available in Stock)
  • Quantity (select Quantity of product approving for lease)
  • Item Code (item code of selected product)
  • Lease Percentage (percentage of lease)
  • Total Lease Amount (Total lease amount to be paid) (Sale price + lease percentage of sale price ( s.p/ l.p*100))
  • Duration (duration of months for which lease is approved)(Lease amount is divide with respect to Duration i-e one month lease, 2 month lease)
  • Advance (any advance paid) (deducts from monthly installment and Total lease amount)
  • Monthly Installment (monthly installment is the amount of lease that is to be paid each month after deducing it from months of lease and Advance paid)
  • Monthly Installment (monthly installment is the amount of lease that is to be paid each month after deducing it from months of lease and Advance paid)
  • First Installment Due Date (Due date on which first installment is to be paid)
  • Remaining installment Due Date (due date of second Installment)
  • Approving Manager (manager who approved and filled the lease advance)
  • Lease type (Fixed balance, Reducing balance)
  • Cash Counter (select cash counter for processing the lease advance)
  • Bank account (select Bank account for processing the Lease advance)
  • Comments (any additional comment)
Note:A plus sign is added to add another Product for lease. It also shows the fields of product as of first product i.e. Name, Sale price, total quantity (in stock), Add Quantity (for lease), Item code.
      After Clicking Save button, Lease case is made and processed.

Received Installment
      For receiving the installments made in lease Advance form, Received installment is used.       First, case no. is selected       After selecting Case no. Following info is shown if the case no. exists:
  • Customer name
  • Product name (product of lease)
  • Monthly installment (each month’s installment)
  • Received Installment (any installment received)
  • Discount (any discount given on lease amount)
  • List of Installments against that case no. with:
  • Previous balance (previous balance of customer)
  • Total Installments (installments divided into months)
  • Principle Payment (total payment due for that month)
  • Lease income (lease amount for that month)
  • After balance
  • Due date (Date for which installment is due)
  • Receiving Date (date for which installment is received)
  • Fine
  • Bill no.
  • Status (pending or received status for any lease installment)
      On clicking the combo box next to status of any Lease detail, the lease installment is selected to receive.
      Fine can also be added on selected installment by:
  • Selecting Fine Type
  • Adding Fine amount
  • Adding Recovery Officer
  • Select Cash Counter / Bank
  • After Clicking Save, Lease installment is processed.
  • Print option is available, on clicking it, the details of lease installment are shown containing Details of Customer (as available on form)
  • Product Details (Name and Id)
  • Date (date of installment received)
  • Recovery officer
  • Lease Installment monthly processed as well as remaining.
  • Fine (if any added) on installment.
  • Bill number
  • Statuses of Each installment.
Early Settlement
      There is also option to opt for early settlement of Lease. Early settlement means settling (paying) all lease installments at one time.
      On selecting early settlement, a model is opened containing all details of customer.
  • Fine Type , fine amount (option to choose to add fine)
  • Cash counter / Bank (counter from which amount is paid)
  • Recovery officer
  • Discount (discount, if any, given to customer on lease amount)
  • Settlement Amount (total amount settled)
  • Principal Amount (total amount minus discount)
  • Lease income (lease income of user)
  • After adding the info, early settlement is processed.
  • Print option is given to print the invoice of early Settlement containing details of Cash counter/ Bank selected, Customer, Case no., Settled amount, Lease amount, Principal Amount, due Date of Lease, recovery officer and discount.
Geo Tag Customer
      In Geo Tag Customer, a customer’s address is checked and verified by viewing his address and going to the exact location to verify the location.
      Adding Geo Tag, user is asked to give access to open live location of user and once user Accepts, it will trace user’s live location. The accessed location is saved against that user’s address.
